Communities Near You

Popular Communities

Emerald City Wanderers

This is a Seattle-based chapter of America’s Walking Club, and our club is known as the “Holiday Club” because we sponsor organized walking events on or near holidays. We host many self-guided year-round-walks in Seattle.

Seattle Singles

This is a singles social events page for singles who enjoy a more outgoing lifestyle through amazing, diverse and cultivated events located in Seattle, WA. Seattle Singles offers many fun and adventurous events and venues for singles of all ages.

Seattle Electric Vehicle Association (SEVA)

SEVA is an all-volunteer group of Seattle-area folks who are passionate about all things having to do with electric cars. We get together to have some plain, old fun at our free monthly meetings.

Washington Trails Association

WTA recognized the need for people whose lives were being impacted by the pandemic to get outdoors, safely and responsibly.

New Communities

The Seattle Vegan Meetup Group

This is an eclectic group of Seattle-area vegans and veg-curious. It's a lot of fun to meet other vegans and we welcome everyone interested in a vegan diet / lifestyle.

Club Seattle Runners Division (CSRD)

This group meets every Thursday evening for a run at your own pace, followed by hangin’ at one of the many local breweries in the area.

Rapha Cycling Club (RCC)

RCC is an international cycling club with a chapter in Seattle. Their Seattle clubhouse is located in Capitol Hill. They host free rides, as well as club rides and other benefits for paid members.

Seattle Language & Culture Meetup

This is a group for anyone interested in any language or world culture. Any level of experience with a language is welcome.
